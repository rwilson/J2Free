Easy Entity Administration
J2Free's admin control panel analyzes the structure of your entities to expose a CRUD interface into your persistence layer, without you having to write a single line of code.
Scalable
An infrastructure built to meet your growing needs and ease your growing pains. JamLegend.com is the largest J2Free deployment on the web, scaling seemlessly to over 1M visits a month.
Less XML
Designed to reduce the crunches for you to get up and running faster. No need to edit a deployment descriptor to map a Servlet. Enjoy annotation based URLMapping for simple, or complex Regex mappings.
